About Us

Learn more about our family-run restaurant.

Family run

Established by our founder Mario in 1975, Mario's Pizzeria has been serving it's loyal customers for almost 50 years. Since we opened, we have aimed to recreate the ethos of Italian home cooking, focusing on serving traditional, rustic food.

Mario's Pizzeria is still run by Mario's family today and we still make our pizzas to the original recipe using authentic stone based ovens and finely selected ingredients all made to order.

The atmosphere at Mario's Pizzeria is all about feeling welcome and part of the family. We welcome everyone from the smallest baby to great grandma and want to ensure you all have an enjoyment, authentic Italian experience.
