Authentic Italian-style

We make our dough on site twice a day with just four ingredients: flour, water, salt & yeast. We double ferment it for a minimum of 20 hours at room temperature which makes it soft, light and easy to digest. All our pizzas are roughly 14” and cooked for just 60 seconds in our stone pizza oven before being sliced and served to your table.

Our pizzas are topped with San Marzano tomatoes, the only tomatoes that can be used for True Pizza Napoletana, our Fior De Latte, semi-soft, fresh cheese made in the style of Italian mozzarella and garnished with oregano, basil and extra virgin olive oil.
